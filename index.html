About Our School

Hope's Peak Academy has been standing tall in the center of this town for as long as the stone age has been over! For decades, we have accepted numerous Ultimates from around the world to give them a lead in a promising future as the new generation of leaders of tomorrow! With our rigorous curriculum, strong faculty, and supportive environment, every student at Hope's Peak has a chance to succeed!

Ultimates

Our students aren't called students, but rather Ultimates! We refer our students as such because of their talents! An Ultimate is a prodigy in any given field or career known to man! Apply!

To Apply to Hope's Peak Academy...you don't! Our Ultimates are scouted by our school's team to determine if they really are the best of the best! Not any average joe gets to step foot here, there's a process! Prior to scouting, the student must be at least 16-years-old and already enrolled in another high school to be considered. However, this only applies to the Main Course. Reserve Course is for students who aren't talented, but they must take an entrance exam to attend. The tuition for these students is 200K a year, with room and board, textbooks, activities, and facilities considered. Scholarships are offered at our school, but they aren't as easily attained, and no outside scholarships are accepted.
